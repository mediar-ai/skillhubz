# Internal Communications

Resources for writing internal communications including status reports, leadership updates, newsletters, FAQs, and incident reports.

## Prerequisites

- Understanding of company communication style
- Access to relevant project/team context
- Knowledge of audience and stakeholders

## Instructions

1. **Identify Communication Type**
   - 3P updates (Progress, Plans, Problems)
   - Company newsletters
   - FAQ responses
   - Status reports
   - Leadership updates
   - Project updates
   - Incident reports

2. **Follow Format Guidelines**
   - 3P Updates: Progress (what's done), Plans (what's next), Problems (blockers)
   - Newsletters: Company-wide updates, achievements, announcements
   - FAQ: Clear Q&A format with concise answers
   - Status Reports: Metrics, milestones, timeline updates

3. **Tone and Style**
   - Professional but accessible
   - Concise and scannable
   - Action-oriented where applicable
   - Appropriate level of detail for audience

4. **Structure**
   - Clear headers and sections
   - Bullet points for easy scanning
   - Highlight key takeaways
   - Include next steps or calls to action

## Error Handling

- If communication type unclear, ask for clarification
- If missing context, request necessary information
- Adapt format to company-specific preferences

## Notes

- Keywords: 3P updates, company newsletter, weekly update, FAQs
- Keep updates focused and relevant to audience
- Include dates and attribution where appropriate
